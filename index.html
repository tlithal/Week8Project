Raise a Suilen

Raise a Suilen is a japanese rock band with a sound that combines rock with electronic, similiar to Linkin Park or Fear and Loathing in Las Vegas.
They are an ingame band, part of the Girls Band Party game, but they also play shows as a live band as well.
