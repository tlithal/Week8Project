Raychell


Raychell is the bassist and vocalist of the band. She has an amazing lower octave tone and an explosive voice that can capture the heart of the audience as well as hype up a crowd
during the band's more energetic songs.
She goes by the stage name Layer and also voice acts that character in the mobile rhythm game Girl's Band Party.
